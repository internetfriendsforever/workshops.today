posters, patterns, programs

10–16 December 2018 Low Standards
Pløens gate 1
Youngstorget, Oslo

A two-day workshop + exhibition introducing programming as a visual design tool

Participants will be introduced to coding concepts like variables, loops, drawing functions, coordinate systems and parameters

Together, we will explore waves, rhythm and contrast through repetition of elementary graphic forms

The work will be printed on A2 posters and exhibited at the end of the week alongside the code used to produce it. All participants will receive copies of the work

applications closed

No prior programming experience required

Participants will be selected based on group diversity (no particular expertise in any particular field required)
